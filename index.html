Our Mission

Junior Coding League is a STEM education initiative that helps elementary and middle school students get exposure to applications of computer science at an early age. Unfortunately our in-person library classes were suspended nationally due to the COVID-19 crisis. Therefore, we are offering virtual instruction through video livestream classes to provide high quality, free, virtual instruction during this global crisis. next level.
