How Does it Work?

Junior Coding League finds able volunteers through our screening process.

Our dedicated volunteers come up with a lesson plan that allows for creative exploration.

Students create applications and machines to solve problems in their communities.

Through these methods, Junior Coding League ensures that all students explore their interests as thoroughly as possible, and that they are able to help their communities in the process. Students develop keen interests in coding, as they can practially apply knowledge, and, in the process, fall in love with the process of coding. Furthermore, students are able to give back to their communities through the unique projects they develop.

Meet our partners!

Humans for Healing is a nonprofit organization that aims to help educate people throughout the world and increaase literacy rates. We work with them in order to make sure that together we are able to combat illiteracy and create as much education as possible. All money we earn is donated to Humans For Healing, and they use it for projects like creating computer labs, libraries, etc.

Start a Chapter With Us!

Chapters are essentially regions in which Junior Coding League operates. Because we cannot personally set up new regions, we encourage highschoolers to reach out to us and help set up a chapter in their region!
