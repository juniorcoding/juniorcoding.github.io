About Junior Coding League

Our Mission


Our mission is to create the entrepreneurs of the next generation. We do this by introducing computer science applications to these students at an early age and providing them with introductory lessons in a variety of programming languages and lessons on how they can apply coding to solve problems.



Who are we?


The teachers of Junior Coding League are composed of four highly motivated high school computer science enthusiasts from New Jersey. You can learn more about us from our Leadership page.



Who are the lessons for?


Our main audience are elementary and middle school students that are looking to improve upon their foundations in computer science.
