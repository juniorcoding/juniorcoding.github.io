About Us

We help our students through hands on learning and firsthand experience. We provide students with opportunities unavailable in classrooms, thus allowing them to prosper.

Rather than focus on block programming, we allow our students to experiment with languages like Python and Java in order to better develop their skills.

How Does it Work?

Junior Coding League finds able volunteers through our screening process.

Our dedicated volunteers come up with a lesson plan that allows for creative exploration.

Students create applications and machines to solve problems in their communities.

Students deploy their solutions into the real world, and the process repeats!
